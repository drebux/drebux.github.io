New Fila Styles Added

New Fila Styles Added

06/05/2018 - From the great athletic brand Fila, we have added 3 news styles from the Runtronic collection.

These slip-resistant styles will help you have secure stable footing on slick, wet floors common in healthcare facilities – one less thing to worry about. The lightweight athletic styling provides support for fast-paced forward and lateral movements that are characteristic of any healthcare professional. A memory foam insole provides conformed comfort and added balance while a CoolMax® sockliner helps keep your feet cool and dry even through the longest shift. A V-notch Achilles heel wrap provides added support and less pressure on the Achilles tendon.

These shoes will help your feet feel good so you can stay focused on doing good.
